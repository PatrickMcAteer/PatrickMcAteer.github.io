Who Am I...

I am an international student currently attending Seton Hill University. I am planning to graduate in May 2022 with a Bachelor of Science in both Computer Science and Cyber Security. Not only am I hardworking in the classroom but I also am a very sports-oriented person, spending 4 years on the men's soccer team here at the university. Outside those I have also been a member of both the SAAC (Student Athletic Advisory Committee) and MISO (Multicultural & International Student Organization) whilst on campus. I have spent most of my life travelling all around the world, living in 5 different countries, England, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, UAE, and USA. Although I am currently in the USA my home is Dubai, where my family also reside. After my time here at Seton Hill, I hope to either find a job here in the USA or go home to the UAE and pursue one there.

I am a passionate, hardworking, and creative person who loves to pursue projects that inspire me. While being here at Seton Hill, I have participated in many classes, from Database Management to C++ programming, Dark web Analysis to Computer Ethics. Providing me with the necessary skills to tackle many areas of the computing sector. Throughout my 4 years, I have found that design, aesthetics, and creativity is the area in which I wish to pursue. That is why a career in UX/UI design is my current goal, Not only am I interested in Computing and the world around it, but also other areas such as: Sports, Photography, Music and more. Sports has always been something I have been tied to, playing Soccer, Golf, Rugby, Cricket, Running and Gaelic Football mostly throughout my life. From here I grew most of my skills, especially teamwork and communication which I believe to be some of my best traits. Not only that but it forced me to push myself like breaking a fastest time on a run or score a goal in a final of a soccer game. This I take with me to my future profession, where I have to come up with new website idea or a different take on an out of date logo.
